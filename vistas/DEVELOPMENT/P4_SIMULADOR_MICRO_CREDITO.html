Acercasa is a company that grants long-term loans, backed by a mortgage guarantee, to finance personal, family and business projects. This simulator, available on the web, is an application that allows clients to calculate the monthly payments that they must make, when they are granted a loan. The parameters for this calculation are: The required value, the financing time and the business conditions.
